The Problem

What AI is doing to your data right now

👁️

Behavioral Tracking

Every click, scroll, pause, and purchase is logged, analyzed, and sold. AI models are trained on your behavior to predict — and manipulate — you.

🎙️

Voice & Face Harvesting

Smart devices and cameras feed your biometric data into AI databases. Your face and voice can identify you anywhere — without permission.

📱

App Data Mining

Free apps harvest your location, contacts, and usage patterns to train AI systems and sell access to advertisers and governments.

🔗

Cross-Platform Profiling

AI stitches together data from dozens of sources to build a profile that knows you better than you know yourself.

🤖

AI Deepfakes

Your photos and videos can generate convincing fake content. AI clones your likeness, voice, and writing style with minimal source material.

☁️

Cloud Exposure

Files in cloud services are regularly scanned and used for AI training. Your private documents are not as private as you think.
